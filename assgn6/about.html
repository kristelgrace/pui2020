about us

bun bun bake shop has been producing the highest quality cinnamon rolls in Pittsburgh since 2020. our secret? we use only the finest quality ingredients imported from Sweden. we are committed to creating the best experience for our customers. our cinnamon rolls are mixed, baked, and served on the same day. taste it for yourself: find us along Forbes Avenue or order online!

our customers have said that they feel like they're in Sweden when they have a taste of our cinnamon rolls. we take pride in cinnamon rolls and serve a limited amount of cinnamon rolls to maintain our quality. if you are not satisfied with your cinnamon rolls, we offer exchanges or full refunds.
